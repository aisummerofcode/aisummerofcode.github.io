#SYNOPSIS2025

"Softare 2.0, Reliable AI Systems, and You"

Generative AI and large language models have ushered in a new era of software. Foundation models are transforming the way software is being developed, and dynamic, goal-oriented AI agents are taking over complex tasks and simplifying user experiences. At #Synopsis2025, builders and innovators in AI will gather to explore this shift, discuss practical aspects of building and scaling complex enterprise systems in the era of AI, and examine the critical role of developers, users and regulators in shaping the AI future.

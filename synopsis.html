Explore the SoTA in AI Engineering

Uncover insights into the latest advances in AI through a broad range of topics that tackle real world challenges

Foundation Models

    Discover the evolution, capabilities and architectures behind today's most powerful AI systems. Understand how multimodal reasoning, retrieval augmentation, finetuning strategies, and model evaluation frameworks are evolving to support enterprise-grade AI systems.

AI Agents: Use Cases

    See how autonomous and semi-autonomous agents are transforming real workflows, from analytics and code generation to customer support, IT automation and research advancement. Learn where agents create the most value today and how enterprises are deploying them safely.

AI Agents: Infrastructure

    Dive into the compute, memory, vector databases, secure sandboxes, tool APIs, orchestration engines, and real-time execution layers that power agentic systems. Understand what modern infrastructure must provide to enable scalable, reliable and governable AI agents.

AI Agents in Production

    Learn the practices required to move agents from prototype to production, including evaluation frameworks, guardrails, monitoring, safety checks, prompt hardening, fallback logic and lifecycle management. Discover how teams ensure reliability at scale.

MLOps / LLMOps

    Understand the pipelines, tools, and operational workflows behind continuous delivery of AI systems. Topics include data orchestration, experiment tracking, deployment strategies, observability, governance and performance optimization for modern AI workloads.

AI in SWE

    Explore how AI is reshaping software engineering, from code generation and automated testing to architecture design, documentation and debugging. Understand how developers integrate AI copilots, agents, and toolchains into high-velocity, high-quality software development practices as we enter the Software 2.0 era.

AI Infrastructure: Cloud

    Discover how modern cloud platforms power scalable AI systems, from specialized AI compute and distributed training to serverless inference, vector databases, and managed orchestration. Learn how enterprises design elastic, cost-efficient and high-performance AI infrastructure.

Reliability & Evals

    Understand the frameworks and methodologies used to evaluate AI systems for accuracy, robustness, safety and performance. Explore techniques for red-teaming, regression testing, scenario-based evaluations and continuous validation to ensure reliable behavior in production.

Security in AI

    Examine the security challenges and protections required for AI systems, including data protection, prompt injection defenses, secure agent execution, and supply chain safeguards. Learn how organizations protect AI workloads end-to-end.

Responsible AI

    Explore the principles and practices for building fair, transparent, and accountable AI. Dive into topics such as bias mitigation, explainability, risk assessment, privacy-preserving techniques and governance processes that ensure AI aligns with human and organizational values.

Enterprise Enablement

    Learn how businesses prepare teams, processes, and culture for AI adoption. From talent development and organizational design to change management and internal tooling, see how enterprises empower employees to build, deploy and scale AI & enterprise systems effectively.

AI Product Management

    Understand how product leaders design, ship and iterate AI-powered products. Dive into evaluation metrics, user feedback loops, AI/UX patterns, risk trade-offs, roadmap planning, and the unique challenges of working with non-deterministic, evolving AI systems.

About AISOC

AISOC was launched in 2024 to fill the growing gap in AI education and democratize access to world-class AI skills. With the rise and prominence of large language models and generative AI, and how these technologies are reshaping the world of software, it is now more important than ever to raise talents that are equipped to build the next generation of AI systems and GenAI-powered applications. And we are taking on this mission!

Through intense, hands-on technical training in the foundations, engineering and applications of large language models and modern AI systems, AISOC stars develop rigorous understanding and mastery of the technical skills needed to build next-gen intelligent applications and push the boundaries of the AI landscape.

Pathways

TRACK I: APPLIED AI

LEVEL - BEGINNER


Learn how to build LLM chatbots, RAG-powered applications, and agentic systems for real-world use cases. Build APIs that interact with frontier LLMs, embedding models and external tools to provide retrieval, reasoning and conversational capabilities.

TRACK II: PRODUCTION AI

LEVEL - INTERMEDIATE


Learn how to take LLMs, RAG applications and agentic systems into production. You will master the engineering best practices for designing, implementing and deploying scalable AI systems through hands-on tutorials, workshops and projects.

TRACK III: FOUNDATION AI

LEVEL - INTERMEDIATE


Dive into the inner workings of foundation models, including LLMs and large reasoning models, from underlying math and architectures to trainning and finetuning techniques to deployment. Prerequisites

APPLIED AI

    Working knowledge of a programming language such as Python, either in the context of data science/analytics or software development. Familiarity with APIs, cloud, and web frameworks is a plus, but not strictly required.



PRODUCTION AI

    Working knowledge of Python or similar programming languages in the context of software development. At least basic familiarity with prompt engineering and LLM APIs such as OpenAI API for prototyping. Prior experience with shell scripting, Docker and cloud platforms such as GCP or AWS is a plus. Familiarity with GPUs, CUDA and HuggingFace is also welcome but not strictly required



FOUNDATION AI

    Working knowledge of Python or similar programming languages in the context of machine learning or software development. Familiarity with deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch or prior experience training traditional machine learning algorithms is a plus but not mandatory. At least basic familiarity with prompt engineering and LLM APIs such as OpenAI API for prototyping. Familiarity with GPUs, CUDA, HuggingFace, shell scripting, Docker and cloud platforms such as GCP or AWS, is a bonus.



Who should attend?

APPLIED AI

    Beginners to LLMs and generative AI systems who want to gain mastery in the development of LLM-powered applications, chatbots, RAG pipelines and agentic systems. Target audience includes, but is not limited to, data professionals, software developers, ML engineers, technical product managers, etc, at any levels.



PRODUCTION AI

    Data professionals, ML engineers and software developers seeking to build production-grade AI systems or integrate LLMs into production data/ML pipelines or software applications. Prior experience building and deploying traditional software, data pipelines or traditional machine learning systems is essential. Familiarity prototyping LLM applications, working with OSS models or LLM APIs, or training/finetuning LLMs is a bonus.



FOUNDATION AI

    Anyone with at least fundamental knowledge building machine learning models or prototyping simple LLM-based applications such as RAG, QA systems and agents, seeking to advance their skills in the training and optimization of large language models for deployment into real-world applications. Mathematically inclined folks with computational experience using Python, MATLAB or similar programming languages but no prior experience working with ML or LLMs are also welcome to participate in this track.
